Install the firmware onto your M5Stack Tab5.

  1. Open this page in Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge on a computer.
  2. Connect the Tab5 with a USB-C data cable.
  3. Click Install, then choose the Tab5's serial port.

Your browser can't flash here — use desktop Chrome or Edge. Serial access was blocked. Allow it and reload.

Not detected? Hold the Tab5's RESET button for ~2 seconds (until the green LED flashes fast) to force download mode, then click Install again.
